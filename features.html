Features

AI Food Scanner

Scan barcodes or take a photo. Our AI recognizes food items, estimates serving size, and suggests nutritional values.

Integration note: Connect a barcode library (e.g. html5-qrcode, QuaggaJS) and an AI image recognition / nutrition API. See js/app.js for placeholders.

Digital Pantry & Expiry Tracking

Add items via scanning or manual input. Pantry lists quantities, purchase dates, and expiry predictions. Get alerts for items close to expiring.

AI Recipe Generator

Generate healthy recipes tailored to your goals and current pantry contents. Recipes come with estimated macros and steps.

Food Journal & Insights

Log foods, view calories and macronutrient trends, and get AI-driven feedback and suggestions to improve adherence to your goals.
